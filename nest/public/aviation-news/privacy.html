Aviation News

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 30 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Aviation News mobile app ("the app") collects, how it is used, and who it is shared with. By installing or using the app you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please uninstall the app.

Who we are

Aviation News is an independent Android application that aggregates aviation industry news from publicly available sources, provides flight tracking data, air quality information, and curated YouTube aviation shorts. The app is distributed free of charge.

Accounts

The app does not require you to sign up, sign in, or create an account. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, date of birth, or any personally identifying information.

What we collect

  • Device & usage data (automatic): Android version, device model, app version, language preference, crash reports, and aggregate usage events (for example, screens opened or buttons tapped). This is handled by Google Firebase (Analytics and Crashlytics).
  • Bookmarks (on your device only): the articles you bookmark are stored locally on your device. They never leave your device.
  • Network requests to our backend: when the app loads content, it contacts our API (aviation.animekill.com). Our servers receive your IP address and request time in their access logs. This is kept for up to 30 days and used only for security/abuse monitoring.
  • Push notifications via Firebase: if you enable notifications, Firebase Cloud Messaging stores a device token to deliver alerts. The token is not linked to your identity.
  • YouTube video playback: when you watch aviation shorts, the embedded YouTube player may set its own cookies and collect telemetry. YouTube's privacy policies apply independently.
  • AQI / flight data queries: when you use air quality or flight tracking features, the app may send your approximate latitude/longitude coordinates to fetch nearby data. These coordinates are kept in server logs for no more than 24 hours.

We do not collect your contacts, photos, microphone audio, SMS messages, call logs, or browsing history.

How the data is used

  • Keep the app running reliably (diagnosing crashes, performance).
  • Understand which features are used, so we know what to improve.
  • Prevent abuse of the backend (rate-limits, bot detection).

We do not sell your data. We do not run third-party ad networks in the app. We do not build advertising profiles about you.

Who we share data with

  • Google Firebase — processes analytics events, crash reports, and push notification delivery on our behalf.
  • YouTube — when you play aviation shorts, the embedded player contacts YouTube directly.
  • News sources (aviationa2z.com, simpleflying.com, and similar) — the app fetches publicly available article data from these publishers. Your device makes requests to our API, not directly to the publishers.
  • Law enforcement — only if compelled by a valid legal request.

Your choices

  • Disable analytics: in your device's system settings you can disable Google Analytics for this app, or uninstall it.
  • Disable notifications: turn off notifications in Android Settings → Apps → Aviation News → Notifications.
  • Clear app data: delete all locally stored data (bookmarks, preferences) from Android Settings → Apps → Aviation News → Storage → Clear data.

Children

The app is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided such information, please email us so we can delete it.

Retention

  • Firebase analytics and crash reports: retained according to Firebase's default retention windows (up to 14 months).
  • Server access logs: deleted after 30 days.
  • Device tokens (push notifications): retained until you unsubscribe or uninstall the app.
  • Location coordinates (AQI/flight queries): deleted within 24 hours.
  • Bookmarks: stored only on your device; deleted when you uninstall the app.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. When we do, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will change. Material changes will also be announced inside the app on the next launch.

Contact

Questions, complaints, or data deletion requests: support@animekill.com
